Brand has room to flex, advanced animation/motion graphics needed, any length.

Strict brand, longer than 10 sec, talent included. Design provides overlays & stickers. Also: flex brand + 3D elements.

EDF — Editor + Heavy Design

Strict brand + advanced animation or 3D. Design provides full storyboards/scenes.
